Incineration can't solve the circularity problem

Waste to energy recovery (or incineration), commonly found in the EU,is used to burn anything that cannot be recycled or recovered, including PET plastic items. While this is a good way to avoid landfills - gases produced during this procedure need to be processed properly to avoid CO2, NOx, SOx pollution, and does not solve the need for increased demands of recycled plastic

THE SOLUTION IS..: ENHANCED RECYCLING

  • ENHANCED RECYCLING
  • 01.
    USAGE / DISPOSAL

Today, only “easy to recycle” plastic items such as bottles or clean packaging are recycled. This accounts for less than 10% of all post consumer plastic waste. The rest is incinerated or landfilled, resulting in pollution and lost raw material.

  • 02.
    DEPLOY

Our enhanced recycling process can recycle ALL PET plastic items including multi-layer items and multi-colour fibers without the need to pre-sort, pre-wash, or separate out other plastics. These items are simply collected and chemically recycled back to the raw monomers.

  • 03.
    PTA & MEG

PTA and MEG - also known as purified terephthalic acid and mono-ethylene glycol are the raw materials used to make PET plastic in the first place. With DePoly's technology, we produced them from PET plastic waste instead of from oil, closing the loop in PET plastic value chain and creating a truly circular economy.

  • 04.
    PRODUCT

The PTA and MEG produced by DePoly possess the same quality and purity as their fossil sourced counterparts allowing the plastic industry to remake virgin quality PET plastic items from DePoly branded recycled TPA and MEG.
